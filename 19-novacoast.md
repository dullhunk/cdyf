# Opportunities with Novacoast {#novacoast}

Novacoast have a Year in Industry placement opportunity: 

* **Job Title**: Penetration Tester Intern
* **Location**: Central Manchester (hybrid – 2 days in office)
* **Duration**: Year in Industry (12 months - full time)
* **Salary**: £27,300 pa

Are you a second-year Computer Science student? Join our Penetration Testing team at Novacoast! As an Intern, you will play a key role in maintaining and developing tools that support our cybersecurity operations. You'll have the chance to shadow live penetration tests, contribute to cutting-edge cybersecurity tooling, and develop Python-based tools that make a real impact for the team.

## Responsibilities: {#novaresp}

* Collaborate with the Penetration Testing team to identify tooling requirements and provide technical solutions.
* Assist in real-world penetration tests.
* Enhance existing tooling for efficiency and scalability.
* Evaluate and integrate open-source tooling.
* Test, debug, and deploy new scripts and tools to streamline penetration testing processes.
* Contribute to documentation for internal tools and processes.

## Required Skills {#novaskills}
* Strong knowledge of Python programming.
* Experience with Git or version control systems.
* Familiarity with software development best practices.
* Basic understanding of cybersecurity principles (or a willingness to learn).
* Good problem-solving and troubleshooting skills.
* Ability to work independently and in a team environment.
* Knowledge of penetration testing tools and techniques is a plus.

## Why Novacoast? {#whynova}
[Novacoast.com](https://novacoast.com) is an international cybersecurity company specializing in IT services and software development. With a 25+ year history in the industry, Novacoast has built, maintains, and assesses IT security solutions for some of the world’s largest private and public organizations. Our team is comprised of hundreds of specialists with backgrounds in IT advisory, development engineering, and managed services.
