# Opportunities with CERN {#cern}

Did you know that CERN employs [ten times more engineers](https://home.cern/science/engineering) and technicians than research physicists, see figure \@ref(fig:cern3-fig). For physicists to understand the data that pours off the [Large Hadron Collider](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Large_Hadron_Collider) (LHC), you need armies of engineers to enable the scientists to do their work. A *lot* of those engineers are working on hardware and software, and most of them won't be physicists. [@hullcern]
