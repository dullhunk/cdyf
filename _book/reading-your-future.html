Coding Your Future: A Guidebook for Students

Chapter 19 Reading your future

If you want to have your future read, you can read about in books, journals, websites, magazines and anywhere else you find the written word.

So get reading your future now. Reading is good for your mind, body and soul. There is no need to gaze into any crystal balls!
