15.2 How do you showcase your passion for technology when you don’t have much coding experience

Personal projects, hackathons (ref), open source, doing courses in your spare time (coursera etc) all showcase your interest.

Your time is finite resource, but anything technical you manage to do over and above the basic requirements of your degree will help your application stand out..

But don’t burn out (ref)
