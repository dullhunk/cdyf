19.7.1 What‚Äôs your story, coding glory?

All your dreams are made, when you‚Äôre chained to the tracker and the software trade, see figure 19.9. What‚Äôs your story, coding glory?

  • How did you find the job and what other jobs did they look for?

19.7.2 Minority report

Inspired by the Philip K. Dick novel (Dick 1956) and it‚Äôs subsequent film, (Spielberg 2002) minority report asks our minorities:

  • What has your experience been at University and in the workplace?
  • How can teaching and learning be made more equitable, diverse and inclusive for members of your minority group?
  • What else can universities do to make campuses more welcoming to members of your minority group?
  • What can employers do to make workplaces more welcoming to members of your minority group?

19.7.4 Time traveller

Do you wish that you knew what you know now, when you were younger? Like Ronnie Lane, Ronnie Wood and Rod Stewart, we all do sometimes, see figure 19.10. So, if you could travel back in time to meet the younger you, just starting University:
