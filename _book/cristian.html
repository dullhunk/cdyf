31 Cristian’s Story

Meet Cristian Bodnar, shown in figure 31.1. Cristian graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science in 2018, this episode was recorded five years later in 2023 on a return visit to Manchester, see section 31.6. As an undergraduate, Cristian did summer internships at Amazon Web Services and Improbable Worlds Limited before doing a PhD at the University of Cambridge and then moving on to Microsoft Research.
