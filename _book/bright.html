18 Opportunities with the Bright Network

The Bright Network has been supporting students and graduates since 2013. There is no specific age their members need to be, but most of the jobs on their website brightnetwork.co.uk are for students and graduates.

18.1 Internship Experience UK

Internship Experience UK (or IEUK) is one example of several opportunities provided by the Bright Network’s every summer, see figure 18.1.

Figure 18.1: IEUK is the Bright Network’s 4-day virtual internship programme taking place every summer opportunities in (1) Technology & Engienering (2) Finance, Professional Services & Consulting (3) Business, Operatings & Marketing or (4) Commercial law. Screenshot from brightnetwork.co.uk/ieuk

In 2025 the experience is designed specifically for first, second and penultimate year undergraduates who are preparing to launch their early career.

Over 145,000 students have completed the live version of IEUK since it began in 2020. It’s a staple part of your university experience — don’t miss out on connecting with like-minded students and 37 industry-leading employers including…

  • Google
  • sky.com, ey.com, deloitte.com
  • JLR.com and britishairways.com
  • cisco.com, IBM, lloydsbank.com
  • www.gchq.gov.uk and www.sis.gov.uk

Find out more about opportunities at brightnetwork.co.uk
